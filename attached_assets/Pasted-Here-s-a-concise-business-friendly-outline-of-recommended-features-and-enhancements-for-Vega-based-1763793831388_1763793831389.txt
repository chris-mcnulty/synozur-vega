Vega Platform: Feature & Enhancement Recommendations
Business Review Outline
1. Features to Prioritize (Bring Forward)
A. Focus Rhythm Integration
Interconnect weekly, monthly, quarterly, and annual review meetings with live progress tracking for Objectives, Key Results, and Big Rocks. 
Add a “Review” module that logs decisions, risks, assignments, and outcomes for each meeting—anchored to company values and strategy. 
B. Company Foundations & Culture
Expand support for company values (title + description), mission, vision, and culture statements. 
Enable tagging of objectives, decisions, and initiatives with relevant values and cultural elements. 
Display culture and brand voice cues throughout the app (e.g., in dashboards, reports, and meeting summaries). 
C. AI-Powered Assistance
Integrate chat-based AI for answering questions, building reports, and acting as an agent (e.g., preparing meetings, nudging owners). 
Ground all AI outputs in each organization’s unique culture, values, history, and brand voice. 
Support an MCP-style agent for safe, auditable automation (with human-in-the-loop controls). 
D. Enhanced Reporting & Snapshots
Enable point-in-time snapshots of OKRs, Big Rocks, and decisions for historical tracking and board/audit reviews. 
Generate branded, values-aligned reports for weekly, quarterly, and annual reviews. 
E. User Experience & Resilience
Maintain per-section error handling so one module’s failure doesn’t break the dashboard. 
Default dashboard views to current quarter, blending quarterly and annual objectives. 
F. Data Portability
Support export/import of Company OS data (including culture, values, and history) for backup, migration, or onboarding. 

2. Features to Consider for Backlog
A. Advanced AI Features
Deeper AI integration for predictive analytics, risk detection, and automated recommendations. 
Expanded agent capabilities (e.g., automated follow-ups, variance detection, policy-driven automation). 
B. Sector-Specific Playbooks
Industry-specific templates and best practices for Company OS, culture, and transformation. 
C. Customizable Branding
Tenant-specific branding, colors, and messaging for deeper personalization. 
D. Governance & Audit
Enhanced audit trails for all AI and user actions. 
Role-based access controls for sensitive data and automation. 
E. Employee Experience Enhancements
Deeper integration with Microsoft 365 (Teams, Outlook, Viva). 
Employee engagement and feedback modules. 

3. Quick Wins & Strategic Impact
Quick Wins:
Values tagging, improved dashboard resilience, and AI chat for Q&A/reporting. 
Strategic Impact:
Focus rhythm integration, culture-grounded AI, and snapshot/reporting features will drive alignment, accountability, and continuous improvement. 

4. Decision Points & Questions
Should “Review” records be required for every meeting, or optional for teams to opt in? 
What level of AI automation is acceptable (fully automated vs. human-in-the-loop)? 
Are there specific industry or regulatory requirements to address first?
